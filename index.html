Privacy Policy

Oneboom respects and protects your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile applications, including Fast Cleanup: Storage Cleaner and our Heart Rate monitoring products.

1. Data Access & Usage

For Fast Cleanup Users:

  • Photo Library: Accessed solely to scan for duplicates/large files locally.
  • Contacts: Accessed only to identify and merge duplicate entries.
  • Biometric Data: Uses system-level Face ID/Touch ID locally. We do not store biometric data.

For Heart Rate Product Users:

  • Health Data (HealthKit): Access to read/write heart rate data for historical trends.
  • Camera Access: Used to detect blood flow changes for calculation.

2. On-Device Processing

Most core functions are performed locally on your device. We utilize Apple's Data Protection technology. We do not upload your private photos, contacts, or health data to our servers.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your personal information or health-related data to any third party. Your data is never used for advertising purposes.
