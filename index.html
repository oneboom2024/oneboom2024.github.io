Privacy Policy

Oneboom respects and protects your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile applications, including Fast Cleanup: Storage Cleaner and our Heart Rate monitoring products.

We adhere to the following principles:

1. DATA ACCESS AND USAGE

Depending on the specific features of the App you are using, we may require access to certain data after you grant explicit permission:

For Fast Cleanup Users:
  • Photo Library: We access photos and videos solely to scan for duplicates and large files to help you optimize storage.
  • Contacts: We access your contact list only to identify and merge duplicate entries.
  • Biometric Data: We use system-level Face ID/Touch ID for the "Private Vault" feature. We do not store or access your actual biometric data.
For Heart Rate Product Users:
  • Health Data (HealthKit): We may access or write heart rate data to the Apple Health App to provide you with historical health trends.
  • Camera Access: We use the camera and flash to detect blood flow changes in your fingertip to calculate your heart rate.
2. ON-DEVICE PROCESSING & SECURITY
  • Local Processing: Most core functions, such as photo scanning and heart rate calculation, are performed locally on your device.
  • Data Protection: We utilize Apple's Data Protection technology to ensure that all sensitive data remains encrypted and secure.
  • No Server Uploads: We do not upload your private photos, contacts, or health data to our servers. Your data stays on your device.
3. INFORMATION SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your personal information or health-related data to any third party.

Your data is never used for advertising or data harvesting purposes.

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement strict security measures and utilize the latest iOS security frameworks to ensure your information is not accessed, used, or disclosed unlawfully.
